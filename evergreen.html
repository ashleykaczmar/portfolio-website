Evergreen

Introduction

As part of the CareerFoundry UI Design Course, I created Evergreen; an e-commerce mobile app that follows a provided brief.

What is Evergreen?

Evergreen is an e-commerce app which features organic, cruelty-free and ethically sourced essential home products, helping sustainability conscious shoppers easily find the products they need.

The Challenge

Many shoppers want to buy organic, cruelty-free and ethically sourced home products. Often, these items are in limited stock at physical stores or are only stocked in specialty stores, which the user may not have easy access to. Evergreen seeks to provide shoppers with an easy way to find and purchase organic, cruelty-free and ethically sourced home products, without worrying about locating and traveling to a physical store.

The Idea

Evergreen is focused on curating all the products our shoppers need so they can easily buy the products they need for a healthy and happy home. A sleek and modern design paired with smart filtering options are key components to the success of the app.
